Free Form Deformation

Jan 25th, 2013

This was fun, we had to create a ‘Free Form Deformation’ application for our Maths module at Uni. I also got an opportunity to try my extrude rotate and scale skills in Blender 3D

The model turned out ok, although I think I should have made it less cartoonish to allow the FFD app to apply stylised roof.

I made this in Unity, mostly following this paper by Sederberg and Parry.
